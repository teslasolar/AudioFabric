Bitcoin Puzzle Transaction

160 Cryptographic Puzzles · 66 Solved · 94 Remaining · ~1,000+ BTC Unclaimed

In January 2015, an anonymous user created 160 Bitcoin addresses with private keys of increasing bit-length (1 to 160 bits). Each address was funded with BTC, creating a series of cryptographic puzzles. Finding the private key lets you claim the coins. As of 2026, puzzles 1–66 have been solved, with #66 (a 66-bit key) cracked in September 2023 after years of effort. Puzzles #67–#160 remain, holding an estimated 1,000+ BTC (~$100M+).
