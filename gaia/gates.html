FULL BODY CIRCUIT BREAKER MAP — THE SAFE SEQUENCE
WHAT HAPPENED: you excited all shells at once without opening gates in order. That's a short circuit — current bypasses the breakers, energy floods unregulated, the system crashes to protect itself. Jisatsu = the fuse blowing. The fix is not more energy. The fix is opening each gate in sequence so energy flows through, not around.

RULE: You cannot open Gate N until Gate N-1 is stable. No skipping. No forcing. Each gate is a one-way valve — energy flows UP only when the gate below is holding.

R0 THE ROOT — GUT / ENS prime 2 · bass · ● · element H

Location: pelvic floor → gut → enteric nervous system (500 million neurons)
Function: survival, safety, physical grounding. "Am I going to die right now?"
Frequency: lowest — subsonic to 60Hz. You feel this, not hear it.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ Haven't eaten in 12+ hours (blood sugar crash)
▸ Sleep debt > 2 nights
▸ Physical danger or perceived danger
▸ Dehydration (electrolytes depleted)
▸ Cold without preparation
▸ Sitting for 6+ hours (pelvic floor disengaged)
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Eat something — real food, not sugar. Protein + fat.
▸ Drink water + salt (electrolytes: K, Na, Mg)
▸ Feet on ground — stand barefoot for 2 min
▸ Kegel squeeze — 10 reps, engage pelvic floor
▸ Deep belly breath × 5 — inflate the gut, not chest
▸ Body scan: "Am I safe right now?" → yes → gate opens
⚠ IF FORCED (bypassed)
Nausea, dizziness, dissociation, panic attack. Body screams "NOT SAFE" and pulls the plug on everything above.
🔧 REPAIR
Lie flat on floor. Eat. Drink. Sleep. Do nothing else until gut settles. 4-24 hours.

R1 THE VAGUS — AUTONOMIC BRIDGE prime 3 · low-mid · 〜 · element Li

Location: vagus nerve — brainstem → throat → heart → gut. The longest nerve. Bidirectional highway.
Function: parasympathetic regulation. "Am I calm enough to process?" Rest-and-digest vs fight-or-flight.
Frequency: 60-150Hz. The hum. Vagal tone.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ HRV below personal baseline (sympathetic dominant)
▸ Jaw clenched, shoulders up, shallow breathing
▸ Cortisol spike (stress, argument, bad news)
▸ Caffeine overdose (adrenals hijacking vagus)
▸ Screen overstimulation without breaks
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Long exhale — inhale 4s, exhale 8s × 6 rounds
▸ Cold water on face (dive reflex activates vagus)
▸ Hum / Om / gargle — vibrate the vagus directly
▸ Unclench jaw — tongue on roof of mouth, relax
▸ Look far away — peripheral vision activates parasympathetic
▸ Sing one note for 10 seconds — any note, sustained
⚠ IF FORCED
Heart rate spikes then crashes (vasovagal syncope = fainting). Cold sweats. The body emergency-brakes.
🔧 REPAIR
Legs up on wall. Slow breathing. No stimulation. Warm drink. The vagus needs to reset its tone — 30 min to 2 hours.

R2 THE GATE — THRESHOLD GUARDIAN prime 5 · gate · ┃ · element B

Location: solar plexus / diaphragm. The literal gate between lower body (survival) and upper body (processing).
Function: permission to feel. "Is it safe to open up?" This is the firewall. Most people's default = LOCKED.
Frequency: 150-300Hz. Transition band. The checkpoint.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ Emotional armor up (protecting from vulnerability)
▸ Diaphragm frozen (chest-breathing only)
▸ Stomach tight / "knot in stomach"
▸ Shame or guilt present (blocks upward flow)
▸ Suppressed crying (energy stuck at diaphragm)
▸ R0 or R1 still locked (cascade lock)
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Diaphragmatic release — lie down, hands on belly, breathe into hands
▸ Twist — seated spinal twist, wring out the solar plexus
▸ Laugh or cry — both use the diaphragm. Either works.
▸ Scream into pillow — controlled release of blocked energy
▸ Say what you're feeling out loud — naming decompresses
▸ Hot shower on solar plexus — heat relaxes the gate
⚠ IF FORCED
Emotional flooding. Uncontrolled crying, rage, or panic. The dam breaks instead of opening. Everything above gets hit with raw unfiltered signal.
🔧 REPAIR
Fetal position. Weighted blanket. Low stimulus. Let the wave pass — it's not dangerous, it's just unfiltered. 1-4 hours. The gate rebuilds itself.

R3 THE HEART — LIMBIC / AFFECT prime 7 · mid · ♡ · element N

Location: heart center, chest, thymus. Limbic system (amygdala, hippocampus).
Function: emotional processing, bonding, love, grief, compassion. "What do I feel about this?"
Frequency: 300-500Hz. The feeling band. Heart coherence at 0.1Hz modulation.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ Chest tight, breathing only into upper lungs
▸ Heartbreak not processed (old grief = permanent lock)
▸ "I don't want to feel this" (voluntary shutdown)
▸ Isolation > 48 hours (no human contact)
▸ Betrayal wound active (trust broken)
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Hand on chest — feel your heartbeat for 60 seconds
▸ Think of someone you love — not desire. Love.
▸ Heart coherence breathing — 5s in, 5s out, focus on heart
▸ Hug someone (20+ seconds activates oxytocin)
▸ Listen to music that moves you — let it in
▸ Say "I feel ___" without explaining or justifying
⚠ IF FORCED
Heart palpitations. Overwhelming emotion with no container. Attachment crisis — love and fear get crossed. Can trigger obsessive bonding or complete emotional shutdown.
🔧 REPAIR
Be with someone safe. Physical contact. Don't talk about it — just be present. The heart gate doesn't repair with words, it repairs with presence. 2-48 hours.

R4 THE VOICE — PFC / EXPRESSION prime 11 · mid-high · △ · element Na

Location: throat, jaw, prefrontal cortex. The executive + expression center.
Function: speaking truth, decision-making, planning, will. "What do I want to say/do about this?"
Frequency: 500-2000Hz. Speech band. Where meaning lives.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ Throat tight, voice small or hoarse
▸ Words stuck — "I can't explain it"
▸ Decision paralysis (too many options = freeze)
▸ Lying or withholding (creates backpressure)
▸ People-pleasing (saying what they want, not what's true)
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Say the unsaid thing — even if only to yourself
▸ Sing — doesn't matter what. Open the throat.
▸ Write it down — pen on paper, stream of consciousness
▸ Make ONE decision — any decision. Small. The muscle needs reps.
▸ Neck stretches — tilt, roll, release jaw
▸ Read aloud — 2 minutes of anything, feeling the resonance
⚠ IF FORCED
Word vomit. Saying things you can't take back. OR complete mutism — the throat locks solid. PFC goes offline = impulsive decisions with no filter.
🔧 REPAIR
Silence. Don't speak for a while. Write instead. The throat gate needs pressure relief, not more output. Hot tea with honey. 1-12 hours.

R5 THE LENS — INSULA / PERCEPTION prime 13 · high · ◐ · element Al

Location: between the eyes + insula (deep cortex). Integration center.
Function: seeing clearly, interoception, pattern recognition, intuition. "What's actually going on?"
Frequency: 2000-8000Hz. Detail band. Where signal separates from noise.
🔒 LOCKED WHEN
▸ Information overload (too much input, can't parse)
▸ Eye strain / screen fatigue
▸ Confirmation bias running (seeing only what you expect)
▸ Dissociation (disconnected from body signals)
▸ Substances that fog perception (alcohol, excess THC)
🔓 UNLOCK PROTOCOL
▸ Close eyes — 2 min of pure dark. Let the visual system rest.
▸ Focus near/far — look at finger, then horizon. 10 reps.
▸ Body scan — eyes closed, feel each body part. Reconnects insula.
▸ Ask "What am I NOT seeing?" — breaks confirmation bias
▸ Nature — fractals in trees/clouds reset visual processing
▸ Cold water on closed eyes — shock-resets the lens
⚠ IF FORCED
Hallucination-like pattern matching. Seeing connections that aren't there. Paranoia. OR total flat perception — everything looks meaningless. The insula can't distinguish signal from noise.
🔧 REPAIR
Sleep. Seriously — the insula repairs during REM. No screens for 2 hours before bed. Sunlight in the morning resets the circadian gate. 8-24 hours.

⚠ IF FORCED (THIS IS WHAT HAPPENED)
Forced R6 = the jisatsu. You pumped current through the crown while lower gates were locked. Energy had nowhere to go. The system reads this as: "I am everything and nothing, I am dissolving, I don't exist" — and the fuse blows. Depersonalization. Ego death without the container to hold it. The safety mechanism is the crash — your body SAVED you by shutting down.

Ground DOWN, not up. Go to R0. Eat. Drink. Touch the floor. Feel your weight. Then R1 — slow breathing. Then R2 — let yourself feel. One gate at a time. R6 repairs last because it repairs itself once everything below is stable. Days to weeks depending on how hard the blow was.

THE RULES — CIRCUIT BREAKER PROTOCOL

RULE 1: SEQUENTIAL ONLY
R0 → R1 → R2 → R3 → R4 → R5 → R6. Always. Every time. No shortcuts.
RULE 2: BOTTOM HOLDS TOP
If R0 fails, everything above cascades shut. If R3 fails, R4-R6 go dark. The foundation holds the structure.
RULE 3: THE GATE TELLS YOU
Locked gate = physical symptom. Nausea = R0. Tight throat = R4. Eye strain = R5. The body is the dashboard. Read it.
RULE 4: REPAIR GOES DOWN
When you crash from R6, don't fix R6. Fix R0. Then R1. Then R2. The crown repairs itself. You repair the foundation.
RULE 5: TIME IS PART OF THE PROTOCOL
R0 = minutes. R1 = minutes. R2 = hours. R3 = hours-days. R4 = hours. R5 = sleep cycle. R6 = days-weeks. You cannot rush a gate that needs time. Trying to = forcing = damage.
RULE 6: EXCITATION IS SAFE WHEN ALL GATES ARE OPEN
The excite protocol works perfectly if you run the gate check first. Current flows through open gates harmlessly. It only burns when it hits a closed gate and has nowhere to go.
