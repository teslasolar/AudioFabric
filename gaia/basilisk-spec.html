5. MANUFACTURING PROCESS — ISA-88 / PACK-ML

5.1 Process Flow

STAGE 1: SOURCING PACK-ML: IDLE → STARTING
Incoming QC: identity (FTIR), purity (HPLC), microbial (USP <61>)
Storage: ALT-711 -20°C, 5-ALA -20°C light-protected, peptides 2-8°C, HA RT
STAGE 2: SYNTHESIS PACK-ML: EXECUTE
2a. Dissolve ALT-711 in pH 7.4 phosphate buffer at 37°C
2b. Prepare GHK-Cu solution: GHK peptide + CuSO₄ → chelation at RT, 30 min
2c. Dissolve retinol in MCT oil carrier (light-protected, N₂ atmosphere)
2d. Prepare peptide cocktail: Matrixyl + Argireline + Syn-Ake + EGF in PBS pH 7.0
CCP-1: pH verification ±0.2 · CCP-2: Cu²⁺ chelation ratio 1:1 ±5%
STAGE 3: ENCAPSULATION PACK-ML: EXECUTE
3a. PLLA dissolved in dichloromethane (DCM)
3b. W/O/W double emulsion with PVA stabilizer
3c. Spray dry: inlet 110°C, outlet 65°C, feed rate 5 mL/min
3d. Wash × 3 with deionized water, lyophilize 24h at -40°C
CCP-3: Microsphere diameter 5-20μm (laser diffraction) · CCP-4: Residual DCM <600ppm (GC)
STAGE 4: FORMULATION PACK-ML: EXECUTE
4a. Dissolve HA (MW 100-300kDa) in water at 20% w/v, stir 12h
4b. Add niacinamide to HA solution
4c. Suspend PLLA microspheres (loaded) in HA matrix
4d. Add peptide cocktail, ALT-711 solution, retinol emulsion
4e. V2 only: Add 5-ALA phosphate (light-protected, last addition)
4f. Homogenize at 3000 RPM, 10 min, vacuum degas
CCP-5: Viscosity 8000-12000 cP · CCP-6: pH 6.8-7.4 · CCP-7: Bioburden <10 CFU/g
STAGE 5: MICRONEEDLE FABRICATION PACK-ML: EXECUTE
5a. PDMS mold preparation: 7×7 array, 500μm depth, 200μm base, 10μm tip
5b. Centrifugal casting: fill mold at 4000g, 5 min
5c. Backing layer: HA 10% w/v, 200μm thickness
5d. Dry under controlled humidity (30% RH) at 25°C, 24h
5e. Demold, inspect, trim to 15mm diameter circular patch
CCP-8: Needle height 500±50μm · CCP-9: Fracture force >0.1N/needle
STAGE 6: QC & RELEASE PACK-ML: COMPLETING
6a. Visual inspection: 100% (automated vision system)
6b. Dissolution test: >80% release in 15 min (Franz cell)
6c. HPLC potency: each active ±10% of label claim
6d. Sterility: USP <71> or validated bioburden + terminal processing
6e. Endotoxin: <20 EU/device (LAL)
6f. Moisture content: <5% (Karl Fischer)
STAGE 7: PACKAGING & STORAGE PACK-ML: COMPLETE
7a. Individual foil pouch (moisture barrier, light-protected), N₂ flush
7b. Desiccant sachet included
7c. Label: lot, expiry, UDI (if device), storage conditions
7d. Storage: 2-8°C (V2 with 5-ALA), 15-25°C (V1 without)
7e. Shelf life target: 18 months (V1), 12 months (V2)
