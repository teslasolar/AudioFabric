This document constitutes the Device Master Record per 21 CFR 820.181 for the BASILISK dissolving microneedle patch.

1.0 Purpose

To establish and maintain the Device Master Record (DMR) for the BASILISK dissolving microneedle patch, containing or referencing all records necessary to manufacture the device in accordance with 21 CFR 820.181. This DMR encompasses device specifications, production process specifications, quality assurance procedures, and packaging/labeling specifications.

2.0 Scope

This DMR applies to all BASILISK patch variants manufactured at the designated production facility. It encompasses the complete manufacturing process from raw material receipt through final packaged product release.

5.0 Manufacturing Process Flow

Seven-stage manufacturing process from raw material preparation through final packaged product.

Stage 1 — Solution Preparation
BSK-WI-001: Dissolve matrix polymers (HA, PVP, CMC) in purified water under controlled temperature. Add trehalose stabilizer. Separately dissolve 5-ALA HCl under amber light conditions. Combine solutions and homogenize.
|
Stage 2 — Mold Filling (Microneedle Tips)
BSK-WI-002: Dispense drug-loaded solution into PDMS microneedle molds under vacuum. Centrifuge to fill needle cavities completely. Remove excess from mold surface. Performed under amber lighting.
|
Stage 3 — Tip Drying
BSK-WI-003: Partially dry tip-loaded molds under controlled conditions (temperature, humidity, airflow, time). Critical: moisture level determines needle mechanical strength and dissolution rate.
|
Stage 4 — Baseplate Casting
BSK-WI-004: Apply backing polymer solution (drug-free or low-concentration) over dried needle tips. Apply backing substrate. Dry under controlled conditions to form integrated patch.
|
Stage 5 — Demolding and Inspection
BSK-WI-005: Demold finished patches from PDMS molds. Perform 100% visual inspection for needle integrity, defects, delamination. Apply release liner. Reject patches failing visual criteria.
|
Stage 6 — Terminal Sterilization / Bioburden Reduction
BSK-WI-006: E-beam or gamma irradiation at validated dose. Alternatively, aseptic processing if radiation degrades active. Sterility testing per USP <71>.
|
Stage 7 — Packaging and Labeling
BSK-WI-007: Place patch in foil pouch with desiccant. Seal pouch (seal integrity verified per sampling plan). Apply label. Insert IFU. Place in secondary carton. Lot coding and serialization.
